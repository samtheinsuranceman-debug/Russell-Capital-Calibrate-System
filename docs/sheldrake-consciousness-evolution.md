# Sheldrake Consciousness Evolution — Intelligence vs. Consciousness Across the Corpus

> **Purpose**: Map Sheldrake's evolving distinction between intelligence and consciousness across his full body of work, and show how it integrates into the Wealth Genome 200-question calibration system + Peter visual layer.

## Pattern Recognition Across the Corpus

### Early Books (1981–1988): Intelligence = Morphic Habits

- **A New Science of Life (1981)**: Intelligence manifests as morphic resonance — habitual patterns of form and behavior transmitted across time and space without material mechanism. Consciousness implied as field-access but not yet explicitly theorized.
- **The Presence of the Past (1988)**: Memory is inherent in nature. Intelligence = the universe's capacity to form habits. Past forms influence present through cumulative resonance, not stored information.

### Mid Books (1991–2001): Intelligence Becomes Alive, Consciousness Extended

- **The Rebirth of Nature (1991)**: Nature is alive, not mechanical. Intelligence pervades all living systems. Consciousness begins to be framed as participatory — the universe is aware.
- **Seven Experiments That Could Change the World (1994)**: Practical, DIY focus. Experiments probe extended mind phenomena (pets knowing owners return, staring detection, phantom limbs, pigeon homing). Intelligence appears as adaptive, resonant pattern-recognition that defies local brain/storage models. Consciousness tested as extended — not confined, capable of influence at distance.
- **Chaos, Creativity and Cosmic Consciousness (2001)**: Trialogues with McKenna & Abraham. Consciousness becomes cosmic, chaotic, and creative — intertwined with novelty, fractals, and evolutionary imagination. Intelligence is not just habit/resonance but generative — the universe's drive toward complexity and new forms.

### Later Books (2012+): Practical Embodiment

- **Science Set Free (2012)**: Challenges materialist dogmas. Intelligence and consciousness are not reducible to brain chemistry. Opens space for extended mind as scientific reality.
- **Ways to Go Beyond (2018)**: Spiritual practices tune consciousness; intelligence measured in health, connection, choice. Practical embodiment of the theoretical framework.
